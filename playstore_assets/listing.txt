APP NAME: Kiwimath

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Fun, adaptive math practice for Grades 1-6 with hints, characters & rewards!

FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max):
Kiwimath makes math fun with 10,000+ questions that adapt to your child's level. Featuring friendly characters like Kiwi, Chikoo, and Captain Kiwi, every problem becomes a mini adventure.

HOW IT WORKS
Questions start easy and get harder as your child improves. If they struggle, the difficulty steps down automatically — no frustration, just steady progress. A smart hint system gives gentle nudges without spoiling the answer.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• 10,000+ carefully crafted math questions across 8 topics
• Grades 1 through 6 — from counting to fractions to logic puzzles
• Adaptive difficulty that matches each child's ability
• 3-level hint ladder for every question — scaffolded support
• Specific feedback on wrong answers — not just "try again" but "12 is too high because..."
• Olympiad-level problems for advanced learners (Felix & Écolier inspired)

TOPICS COVERED
• Counting & Number Sense
• Arithmetic & Operations
• Patterns & Sequences
• Logic & Reasoning
• Spatial Thinking & Geometry
• Shapes & Measurement
• Word Problems
• Puzzles & Brain Teasers

FUN CHARACTERS
Meet Kiwi the curious bird, Chef Cheetah who loves cooking with fractions, Builder Bee who measures everything, and many more! Characters make problems relatable and turn math into storytelling.

REWARDS THAT MOTIVATE
Earn XP, collect badges, and level up through 10 stages. Streak bonuses encourage daily practice. Every correct answer feels like a win.

FOR PARENTS
Track your child's progress with a parent dashboard showing strengths, areas for improvement, and learning patterns. See which topics need attention and celebrate milestones together.

DESIGNED FOR CHILDREN
• No ads — ever
• No data collection
• COPPA compliant
• Works offline for practice sessions
• Clean, distraction-free interface
