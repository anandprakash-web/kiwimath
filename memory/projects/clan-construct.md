# Clan Construct (from Vedantu ISL 2017-18)

**Source:** Clan Construct document by Anand Prakash (Vedantu ISL era)
**Status:** Planning for Kiwimath adaptation
**Also called:** "Clan Wars", "ISL Clan Wars"

## Original ISL Concept

### Clan Formation
- Any student can CREATE a clan
- Joining is INVITE-ONLY (via link shared by existing member)
- Clans are CATEGORY-SPECIFIC (grade/level locked)
- If a student from a different category tries to join → blocked
- Leaderboard and points are scoped to the category

### Point System (Two Types)

**Brain Points:**
- Aggregate of points earned by clan members in Daily Challenges
- Only TOP 10 members' scores count per challenge (prevents gaming with inactive accounts)
- Points refresh after every "Challenge Bust" (challenge completion/expiry)
- Example: 20 members, 18 attempt, top 10 scores summed = 8400 pts

**Brawn Points:**
- Based on clan SIZE at time of challenge bust
- Formula: X points (e.g., 50) × number of members at bust time
- Incentivizes recruiting: grow from 20→30 members = 30×50 = 1500 bonus pts

### Picture Unravel Challenge (Clan Exclusive)
- A mystery image is hidden behind a grid of blocks/pixels
- Earning Brain Points reveals pixels progressively
- Points earned ONLY from challenge launch (not lifetime total)
- Each challenge creates its OWN leaderboard

**Pixel Reveal Algorithm:**
- Each pixel assigned a unique sequential number (1 to n)
- Pixels revealed in ascending order based on points earned
- Same reveal pattern for ALL clans (fair comparison)

**Answer Submission:**
- Only the CLAN LEADER can submit the answer (guess what the picture is)
- Answer type: numerical or text
- Leader can update answer anytime
- Points decrease over time (Day 1 = 10000, Day 7 = 4000) → rewards early correct guesses

**Challenge Winner = highest total of:**
- a) Brain Points (for this challenge)
- b) Brawn Points (for this challenge)  
- c) Answer points (based on day answered correctly)

### Leaderboards
1. **Overall Clan Leaderboard** — lifetime points
2. **Picture Unravel Challenge Leaderboard** — per-challenge, resets each new challenge

### Prizes (ISL era)
- Clan Group Photo
- Goodies
- Award at ISL function

### Invite Flow (ISL era)
1. User sends invite via FB, email, WhatsApp
2. Invitee clicks CTA → lands on page with clan name + registration form
3. Post-registration → redirected to clan page

### Communication Templates
- Clan creation push email
- Confirmation to creator (with invite CTA)
- New member notification to leader
- Invite text (email, WhatsApp, FB)
- "You've been added" confirmation
- Day-after nudge to invite more members

### Collaborative Discussion (CRITICAL — from original pitch)
- Clan members DISCUSS and BRAINSTORM the Picture Unravel answer together
- ISL had a "question discussion stream" (like a chat/forum within the clan)
- Members debate what the image might be, converge on an answer
- Only the Clan Leader inputs the final answer — but the TEAM figures it out
- This collaborative puzzle-solving is the CORE social engagement, not just points
- "Clan members will have to discuss among themselves, brainstorm and conclude"

### Main Test Integration (ISL era)
- Clans also competed in the Main ISL Test (not just Picture Unravel)
- Winning clans got special prizes at the Grand ISL Award Ceremony
- This means clan points could come from BOTH daily challenges AND the big test

### Marketing Framing (ISL era)
- Pitched as "geekiest war of brains" — competitive but fun
- "Invite your friends (and foes... well why not!)" — inclusive tone
- Bragging rights: winning clan photo featured to "lacs of students"
- Prizes kept as surprise to build curiosity

### Picture Unravel = Olympiad Puzzle (CRITICAL CLARIFICATION)
- The image IS the puzzle — NOT "guess what the picture shows"
- Olympiad-level math/logic puzzles: magic squares, cryptarithmetic, logic grids, pattern sequences
- Designed to REQUIRE group collaboration — no single kid can solve alone
- The MOAT is that puzzles take TIME (days of collective reasoning)
- Different members crack different parts → spatial thinkers, number crunchers, logic trackers
- Partial reveals give partial information → can start reasoning at 30-40% visible
- Daily quiz scores feed into clan points as WEIGHTED MEAN (quality over quantity)

## Key Design Principles (from Anand)
- "Engagement like Roblox yet not harming kids"
- Social proof via group achievement, not individual shaming
- Recruitment mechanic (Brawn Points) creates organic viral growth
- Top-10 scoring prevents free-riding while not punishing small clans
- Picture Unravel = curiosity-driven engagement loop
- Time-decay answer scoring creates urgency without stress
- **Collaborative answer-finding is the real social glue** — not just parallel play
